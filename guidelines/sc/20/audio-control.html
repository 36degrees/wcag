Audio Control

A

For any audio on a Web page that (1) plays automatically, and (2) last for more than 3 seconds, at least one of the following is true:

Pause or Stop
A mechanism is available to pause or stop the audio, independently from any other audio output.
Volume Control
A mechanism is available to control audio volume independently from the overall system volume level.

Since any content that does not meet this success criterion can interfere with a user's ability to use the whole page, all content on the Web page (whether or not it is used to meet other success criteria) must meet this success criterion. See Conformance Requirement 5: Non-Interference.
